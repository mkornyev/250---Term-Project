Our wallets are water-resistant. If you ever accidentally spill water on them, just let it dry out! The non-slip interior will prevent water from soaking through into your goods.

Many of our wallets contain RFID protection, which prevents potential thieves from stealing your credit card information by scanning the outside of your wallet!

Our wallets come in a variety of different colors and form factors. There is a Big Skinny wallet for any customer!

The inside of each Big Skinny wallet is composed of Nylon Micro-Fiber in order to keep the wallets as thin as possible.
